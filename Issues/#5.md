Issue Description:
I am experiencing an issue where Minecarts and Boats are still being removed by the auto-removal task, even when a player or NPC is riding them. I have tried multiple filter combinations (!isMounted, !hasPassenger, and adding them to the area-filter), but the entities are still cleared during the execution.

Server Version: Purpur 1.16.5 (Build 1171)
Plugin Version: ClearLag v3.2.2 (or your current version)

Steps Taken to Resolve:

Set boat: false and minecart: false in the auto-removal section to prevent global removal.
Added Boat !isMounted and Minecart !isMounted to the remove-entities list.
Added Boat !hasPassenger and Minecart !hasPassenger as a fallback.
Included Minecart and Boat in the area-filter and kill-mobs filter to protect them from other cleanup tasks.
Verified that no other plugins are performing the removal (the removal message is definitely from ClearLag).
Why I think it's not working:
On modern forks like Purpur 1.16.5, the way entity metadata or "mount" states are handled might differ from older Bukkit versions. It seems ClearLag's internal check for passengers is returning false (no passenger) even when a player is sitting in the vehicle, or the specific entity-type removal in the auto-removal list is ignoring the metadata tags entirely.
